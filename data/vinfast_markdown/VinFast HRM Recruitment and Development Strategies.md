2.1.1 HRM definition, Objective of HRM in Vinfast
Human  resource  management  (HRM)  is  the  procedure  for  hiring
employees,  training  them,  compensating  them,  creating  policies
regarding  them,  and  building  retention  strategies  (University  of
Minnesota, 2016)
